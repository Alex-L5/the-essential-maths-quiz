How to play

Welcome to the quiz! Answer each question and try to get the highest score. Click on Play to start, then answer the questions that appear. Select one answer out of 4 choices. The right answer generates 10 points, while there are no points for an incorrect answer. After you have completed the quiz, the High Score section shows the participants' scores of 10 or more points after up to 5 attempts. To check how you scored, you can enter your username and save it in the input box. You also have the option to start the quiz again. You can also click the buttons at the top left, i.e. Home, Start Game, High Scores and End Game to move through the quiz sections.
